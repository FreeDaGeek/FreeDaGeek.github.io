ABOUT ME

I'm just a broken crayon that chooses to color outside the lines of my limitations.

Annette Paul


Annette Vivid is a south Florida native and a lover of all forms of art whether music, poetry, paintings or theater. She appreciates each creative aspect as it reflects our original Creator in Heaven. She is a wife, mother, sister and friend. Previously serving as a High School Student Ministry leader at her home church, Church by the Glades, Annette enjoys teaching, training and mentoring youth to discover their true potential. Overcoming many obstacles and being gifted poetry with unique performances, Annette is now able to convey a message of hope, forgiveness and a life of freedom as she tells a transformational story and captures the hearts of the audiences. She has performed at many events, sharing stages with Ezekiel, Janette...kiz, Jackie Hill Perry, Preston Perry and much more.
